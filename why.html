So you wanna be the local Smash heel.

The primary draw to maining Incineroar is her personality. Flexing on everyone after everything is a power move hard to achieve otherwise. Plus, in an online metagame where regular taunts are disabled, Incineroar is a step above the rest of the game.

Sicko mode

Another big factor is the ease of play. Once you get past an initial period of adjusting to a slow character, Incineroar's combo game is less complex than other characters but quite rewarding.
